Construction machinery

Safety, comfort and ergonomics are the attributes that best describe working with a SANY excavator. The comprehensive range of SANY products covers mini excavators through to demolition excavators, all equipped with the latest technology and boasting maximum efficiency.

Mobile harbour machinery

SANY mobile harbour machinery stands for sturdy and durable technology that will cope with your constant turnover of containers and heavyweight cargo. SANY offers a comprehensive range of reach stackers (full container loaders), empty container handlers and heavy-duty forklift trucks in a variety of sizes.
